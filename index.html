About

Vijay P. Javvadi is an independent researcher focused on advancing artificial intelligence driven approaches to software testing and intelligent quality engineering. His work explores how machine learning, repository analytics, and intelligent automation systems can transform traditional software testing into predictive quality engineering platforms.

With more than 18 years of experience in software quality engineering and test automation, his research focuses on building intelligent testing systems capable of predicting software defects, generating automated test scenarios, and improving software reliability in large-scale enterprise environments.
