Vijay Prasad Javvadi

Independent Researcher in AI-Driven Software Quality Engineering
My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software quality engineering. The goal of this research program is to develop intelligent systems capable of predicting software defects, improving automated testing efficiency, and enabling data-driven decision making in modern software engineering environments. With over 18 years of experience in software testing and quality engineering, this research initiative aims to bridge the gap between academic research and real-world enterprise software systems.

Research Areas

AI-driven test automation, machine learning–based defect prediction, autonomous testing systems, and intelligent software quality analytics.

Research Program

A multi-year research initiative exploring predictive software quality engineering through repository analytics and machine learning.

Publications

Research papers investigating AI-driven testing frameworks, defect prediction models, and intelligent reliability systems.

Research Impact

Advancing software reliability by applying predictive analytics and intelligent automation to modern software engineering.
